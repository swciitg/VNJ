About Us

IIT Guwahati brings you the seventh edition of its much-awaited sporting extravaganza, SPIRIT. It is the largest sports fest of North East India. This four day premiere sporting carnival kicks off from 26 September and concludes on 29 September. SPIRIT is a great platform for college teams to exhibit their exceptional sporting spirit, and compete for glory!
